Meatballs Page of Favorite things

Hello, My Name is Meatball

I am a 7 year old pittie, who lives with my mom and dad on a farm with cows, chickens, ducks and goats! I was adopted by my parents when I was just 3 months old.
I was adopted from a rescue in Texas. All my siblings and even my mommy were adopted.

Here are a few things that are my favorites.

  1. Toys - I love soft toys with squeakers
  2. Beds - I love many different kinds of beds, dog beds, people beds, and even couch beds
  3. Treats - I am a little picky about my treats, but if you give me something yummy, I will nicely gobble it up

Thank you for stopping by my site, stay tuned for further information about me.
